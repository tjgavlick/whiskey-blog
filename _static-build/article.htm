On the Consumption of Alcohol

And the Oddity of the Hobby

Tasting and collecting whiskey is an odd hobby.

If a guitarist plays too much, her fingers hurt. When a mineral collector sees one too many things he's crazy about, he's going to need to cut back on luxuries next month. But when an alcohol enthusiast goes too deep, he might die. That sucks.

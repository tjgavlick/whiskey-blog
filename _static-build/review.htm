Rittenhouse

25-year Rye

  • Distilled by Heaven Hill
  • 100 proof
  • $200 retail price
  • Type: Rye
  • 25 years old
  • Rarity: Not happening
  • Mashbill: Barely-legal rye (51% rye, 35% corn, 14% malted barley)

This is my favorite drink. This is where I'm coming from with the rest of my reviews; it's the yardstick that I think other drinks should strive to measure up to. I'll update this when something better comes along, but it might be a while.

Back in the eighties, as the story goes, a private customer bought 95 barrels of Rittenhouse rye from Heaven Hill, to be aged on-premises. Fortunately for us, the customer either didn't want the barrels after all, or they never showed up to claim them until they were over-aged. Whatever the case, Heaven Hill decided to offer them up for sale. Nice.

That's great for us — not many hyper-aged ryes tend to make it to market. The ones that do are almost universally excellent.

The 25-year is the last-released of the stocks from this set, following 21- and 23-year expressions. All the barrels were aged on floor 1 of rickhouse 00.

Nose (light, oaky, brown sugar, caramel, fennel)
